Transfer a Charge to a Different Folio

To transfer a charge to a different folio window:

  1. From the Dashboard screen, tap Due Out.

  2. From the Search screen, enter a guest last name or confirmation number and tap the Search button.
  3. On the Lookup Results screen, tap the View Folio button.

  4. On the View Folio screen, tap the Transfer Posting button.

  5. On the Transfer Posting screen, locate the folio window you want to move a charge from. Under the "Move To" column, locate the charge to move, tap the down arrow and choose the folio window to move the charge to. The charge will be posted to the selected window when you return to the previous screen.

    Tip. If you want to move to another window that you do not see listed, then choose another number from the move to list then what is listed and it will automatically create the new window number.
