House Status

This provides a good overview of the Hotel Room Status for a specific day. This is based on the House Status feature in OPERA and should be the same search criteria as entered in OPERA, the business date will be the same as the one inputed in OPERA House Status.

To change the results in OPERA2Go change the search criteria in the OPERA House Status screen.

To access this feature go to the Main Menu of OPERA2Go, select the House Status menu option.

Step 1. Select a Room Type, Room Class, or a specific Date, then (search) or Fetch House Status.

Step 2. The screen displays with an overview of the house status for the day. There are areas on the screen for Room Summary, House Activity, End of Day Projection and the Housekeeping Room Status.
