Component States

Most of the components that are used for experiences have three different states: incomplete, complete, and active. Incomplete components are drawn with a red outline and usually represent newly dragged in components that have not had their properties customized for the experience. Incomplete components can be completed by customizing the contents of their property panes and saving them. Completed components will be drawn with a blue outline, which indicates they have been customized but are not yet active. Completed components are activated through a selection in their properties pane. Once activated, a component is drawn with a green outline.

User groups are the only components that do not have a separate completed state. After an incomplete user group is customized and the customization is saved, the user group immediately becomes active and is drawn with a green outline.
