Using Mobile Sites

Note. Keep in mind the homepage can be customized and may not look anything like the out of the box version documented here.

webMobile (myStayManager) is the MICROS Commerce Platform tool to assist in building your Mobile Hotel website.
In webMobile there are specific functions related to typical tasks a Guest could do on the Mobile Hotel website:

  • webMobile Homepage
  • Make a Reservation
  • Check (Guest) Messages
  • Order Room Service
  • View Guest Profile
  • Guest Loyalty Program with Points and Rewards
  • Property Information Page
