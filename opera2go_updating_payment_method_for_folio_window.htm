Update Payment Method for a Folio Window

To update the payment method for a folio window

  1. From the Dashboard screen, tap the Due Out, In House or the Reservation icon.

  2. From the Search screen, enter a guest last name or confirmation number and tap the Search button.
  4. On the Lookup Results screen, tap the View Folio button.

  5. On the View Folio screen, select the window for which you want to update the payment method. Tap the window to expand its section and tap the Pay button.

  6. On the Pay Reservation screen, tap the Payment Method down arrow and choose a new payment method from the list. For a credit card, enter the card number, the name on the card and the expiration date. Enter an amount and tap the Pay button.
