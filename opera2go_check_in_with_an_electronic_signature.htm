Check In with an Electronic Signature

When the Enable Signature Capture parameter is enabled, during the check-in process the Guest will have the ability to sign their registration card electronically using a mobile device.

To Activate Electronic Signature Capture:

In Site Manager, go to Site Settings > Manage Sites > select the site and choose the Front End Settings > then select the Opera2Go Site Settings tab.

To Check-in a Reservation Using an Electronic Signature:

  1. From the Dashboard screen, select Due In.
  2. On the Lookup Results screen, from the list of Reservations, locate the reservation you wish to check-in and select the Check-in icon next to it.

    Tip: The status of each room will be color coded by the room number (see key below).

    Red = Dirty

    Yellow = Pickup

    Green = Inspected

    Blue = Clean

  3. On the Check-in Reservation screen, using a stylus, select the pencil icon in the Registration Card section.

  4. Using a stylus, the Guest signs their signature on the Signature line of the registration card and selects Check-in.

    Note: If a credit card reader is attached to your tablet device, you can also choose to swipe the credit card at this time by selecting the Swipe Credit Card button.
